Rakhiya Badi

How to Cook Rakhiya Badi

Ready in 5 minutes | Easy & Tasty Recipe

Step-by-Step Recipe
1

Heat Oil

Heat 1–2 tsp oil in a pan

2

Add Onion & Spices

Fry 1 onion, 1 tomato and spices until golden brown

3

Add Badi & Water

Add Rakhiya Badi and ½ cup water. Cover and cook for 3–5 minutes

4

Serve

Serve hot with roti or rice

Tips

✔ Add masala for extra taste

✔ Do not overcook
