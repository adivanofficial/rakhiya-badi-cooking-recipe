How to Cook Rakhiya Badi

Ready in 5 minutes | Easy & Tasty

Start Recipe

1. Heat Oil

Heat 1–2 tsp oil in a pan.

2. Add Onion & Spices

Fry onion, tomato and spices until golden brown.

3. Add Badi & Water

Add badi and cook for 3–5 minutes.

4. Serve

Serve hot with roti or rice.
