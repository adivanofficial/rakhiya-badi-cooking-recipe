How to Cook Rakhiya Badi

Ready in 5 Minutes | Easy & Tasty Recipe

Step-by-Step Recipe

1. Heat Oil

Heat 1–2 tsp oil in a pan.

2. Add Onion & Spices

Fry onion, tomato & spices till golden.

3. Add Badi & Water

Add badi + water and cook for 5 min.

4. Serve

Serve hot with roti or rice.
